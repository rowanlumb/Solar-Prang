Solar Prang

A custom solar-panel electroplating fixture designed to resemble a Buddhist Prang structure and bring "slow-tech" ambiance to your porch. Download the STL files or explore the assembly interactively in your browser.

  • Project Type Solar Decorative Fixture
  • CAD Autodesk Fusion
  • Visualization Three.js
  • Manufacturing FDM 3D Printing
  • Finishing Copper Electroplating
  • Author Rowan Lumb
  • Status In Progress

Comments & Lessons

  • This fixture came out a bit stubbier than intended so could extend the bottom and middle sections probably another total 50mm or so to improve the overall look.
  • Overall product would be improved by making it a warm light that turns on at night so the solar panels are doing useful work.
